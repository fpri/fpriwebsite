TMJ ARTHROSCOPY

Learn about this wonderful
minimally invasive procedure

TMJ Arthroscopy is a minimally invase procedure done in the temporomandibular joint under general anesthesia that prometes healing, movement and greatly reduces pain.
It's recovery time is about three days while physical therapy is done to gain strength in ligaments, muscle and movements that has been lost due to pain and malfunction.
